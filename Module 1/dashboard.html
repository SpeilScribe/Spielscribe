Welcome to SpielScribe

Digital prescription has tremendous potential to change the way we approach patient care and to enhance the overall quality of care. Our application is a step towards overall betterment in healthcare industry.

Our application records the doctor's speech and extracts useful information based on keywords. Keywords are mapped with the information and a digital prescription is formulated. The doctor can preview his prescription for any erroneous information before adding his digital signature. Once the signature is added the prescription is ready to be sent to the patient via emails, sms or through whatsapp. Each prescription is also automatically stored in doctor's space for any future review.
